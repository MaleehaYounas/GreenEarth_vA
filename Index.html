Meet Lucia: A Guardian of the Forest

When Lucia's ancestral forest in the Amazon was threatened by illegal logging, she didn't stand by silently. At just 19 years old, she organized her community and reached out to GreenEarth for support.

Together, we implemented sustainable forestry practices, empowered indigenous guardians, and successfully protected over 50,000 acres of pristine rainforest that serves as home to thousands of species found nowhere else on Earth.

Lucia's story is just one of many where passion and partnership have created lasting change. With your help, we can write more success stories like hers.
